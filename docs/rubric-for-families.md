# Squad Progress Rubrics (Families and Coaches)

The hub can show **monthly progress rubrics** for swimmers in squads where we track skills and habits against a fixed checklist. It is meant for steady feedback alongside race times.

---

## Squads that use this rubric

Seven squads use the pathway from starters through to the performance stream. Typical age bands guide placement; coaching staff confirms where each swimmer sits.

| Squad | Typical ages |
|--------|----------------|
| Pups | 5 to 6 |
| Development 1 | 7 to 8 |
| Development 2 | 8 to 9 |
| Development 3 | 10 to 11 |
| Bronze | 11 to 13 |
| Silver | 12 to 14 |
| Gold | By selection |

Club staff assign and reassign squad coaches in the hub. Only those rostered assignments (including the swimmer’s named coach where set) unlock **Add / Edit evaluation** on the Rubric screen.

---

## Squads **without** this rubric

Some swimmers sit in umbrella squads used for billing or scheduling (such as generic Development, Elite, or Masters). Those squads do **not** show a Rubric tab in the hub. That is normal.

---

## If you coach

1. Open the swimmer’s **Performance** screen.
2. Open **Rubric** when it appears. If **Add / Edit evaluation** shows, you may save that month’s rubric for this swimmer.
3. Pick **month** and **year** (often the current month).
4. For each line, tap **1** to **4**, or **N/A** where it does not apply. **Only checklist scores that are saved appear in Progress History.** Attitude-only saves do not yet add a month row on their own.
5. Add **coach observation** and **swimmer self-report** attitude (each 1 to 5) if you track them that month.
6. Tap **Save evaluation**. Progress History updates for that month straight away without a full page refresh.

---

## If you are a parent

1. Open your swimmer’s **Performance** screen and tap **Rubric** when present.
2. Under **Progress History**, pick the month you want.
3. You see badges for skills and habits the coach rated and attitude where saved. Saving rubrics stays with assigned coaches only.

Secondary parents linked through the hub’s family sharing see the same swimmers as the primary parent.

---

## If something looks missing

- **No Rubric tab:** The swimmer’s squad may not use this rubric in the hub.
- **No Add / Edit card:** Your coach account may not be assigned to this squad or swimmer in the hub.
- **Empty month list:** At least one checklist line must be saved for that calendar month before it appears in history.
