OTTERS SWIMMING KENYA

**Squad Rubrics**

A reference for monthly coach evaluation. Each swimmer is assessed
against the milestones for their current squad.

**How we rate**

**Skills and habits --- each milestone is rated monthly:**

  ----------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Rating**   **Level**           **Definition**
  ------------ ------------------- -------------------------------------------
  **1**        **Not yet           The swimmer has not yet been taught or
               introduced**        attempted this milestone.

  **2**        **Learning**        Attempting the milestone, inconsistent ---
                                   can do it on a good day but not reliably.

  **3**        **Consistent**      Can do it reliably in practice, week after
                                   week.

  **4**        **Mastered**        Can do it under fatigue or in competition,
                                   not only in ideal conditions.

  **N/A**      **Not applicable**  An injury, medical condition, or other
                                   permanent factor excludes this milestone.
                                   Excluded from averages, not counted as
                                   zero.
  ----------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Attitude --- captured separately from skills and habits:**

  ---------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Rating**   **Level**
  ------------ --------------------------------------------------------------
  **1**        Very unhappy

  **2**        Unhappy

  **3**        Neutral

  **4**        Happy

  **5**        Very happy
  ---------------------------------------------------------------------------

*Captured monthly. Both coach observation and swimmer self-report are
recorded. Divergence between the two is useful signal, not a problem to
smooth over.*
