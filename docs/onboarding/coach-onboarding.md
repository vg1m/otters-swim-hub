# Coach onboarding — one page

**Otters Swim Hub** · For pool coaches

---

## 1. Get in

- Log in with credentials from the club admin
- You land on the **Coach dashboard** (`/coach`)

---

## 2. My swimmers

- See swimmers in **squads you are assigned to**
- Use search/filter by squad
- **Family & contacts** — view parent phone, email, emergency contact, and shared-access family (read-only)

---

## 3. Sessions and attendance

- **My schedule** — upcoming sessions for your squads (recurring sessions show each week)
- Open a session from the admin calendar link or attendance URL with the correct **date** for recurring series (`?date=YYYY-MM-DD`)
- **Mark attendance** per swimmer for that session date
- Attendance syncs to the parent’s attendance calendar

---

## 4. Performance and rubrics

- From a swimmer row, open **Progress** (if linked) for times and coach notes
- **Rubrics** — monthly skill/habit ratings where enabled for the squad

---

## 5. Notifications and messages

- **Notifications** — assignments, session pay, club announcements, coach messages
- **Broadcast** (when enabled) — send tips or links to parents in your squads, or messages to other coaches

---

## 6. Who to contact

- **Roster / squad changes:** club admin
- **Session times or pool:** club admin (Sessions in admin calendar)
- **Pay or rate questions:** club admin
- **App login:** admin or technical support via the club

---

*Add your club logo and screenshots offline before printing.*
