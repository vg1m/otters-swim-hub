# Parent onboarding — one page

**Otters Swim Hub** · For parents and guardians

---

## 1. Get in

- **Sign up** at the website → verify your email → **Log in**
- Use the **same email** you used when registering swimmers so everything links automatically
- **Dashboard:** your home screen after login

---

## 2. Your swimmers

- Register new swimmers from the dashboard (or you may have registered before creating an account)
- Each swimmer shows **status** (pending until approved / paid)
- Open **Progress and attendance** for schedules, attendance calendar, and coach notes

---

## 3. Training schedule and attendance

- **Dashboard** shows upcoming sessions for your swimmer’s squad
- **Attendance tab** (on swimmer progress): colour calendar — green = attended, red = missed past session, blue = upcoming
- Schedule updates when the club changes sessions; check **Notifications** for club news

---

## 4. Invoices and payments

- **Invoices** — view amounts, due dates, pay online (card / mobile money) or as instructed by the club
- Download **receipts** after payment

---

## 5. Notifications and feedback

- **Notifications** (nav) — club announcements, invoices, approvals, schedule changes
- **Feedback** — send a message to the club admin; read replies in the same place

---

## 6. Settings and family access

- **Settings** — update your profile, emergency contact, media consent
- **Shared access** — invite a co-parent/partner with their own login to see the same swimmers and invoices

---

## 7. Who to contact

- **Billing or registration:** club office (use Feedback in the app for a written trail)
- **Training / attendance:** your swimmer’s coach via the club
- **Technical login issues:** try forgot-password first, then club admin
